about us

Welcome to College Mania

In the year 2018,College Mania was established with an aim to accumulate all the colleges under a single platform. We are basically a college application approval system working independently with the feature of various amenities such as scholarships,etc. Here any student could login to view the colleges of their own choices. They could even apply for admission and request for the brochure of the same college. He/She could view the counselling dates or interview dates of any college and the cutoffs of all colleges just through a single LOGIN.
